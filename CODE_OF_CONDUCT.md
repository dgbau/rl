# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Accept feedback gracefully and give it kindly
- Focus on what's best for the project and community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainers directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct may be removed from the project.
